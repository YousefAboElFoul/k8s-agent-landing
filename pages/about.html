About K8s Self-Healing Agent

Solving Kubernetes reliability so you can focus on innovation

Our Story

K8s Self-Healing Agent was born from frustration. Our founding team spent years managing large Kubernetes clusters, and we were tired of the constant fire-fighting. Pods would crash at 3 AM. Developers would get paged. Hours would be spent digging through logs and Stack Overflow to find the root cause.

We knew there had to be a better way. We started building tools to automatically detect and fix common pod failures. Within weeks, our team's on-call burden dropped by 80%. Within months, we realized other teams faced the same problem‚Äîso we decided to build K8s Agent as a product.

Today, K8s Agent protects hundreds of Kubernetes clusters, from small startups to large enterprises. We've helped teams save thousands of engineer-hours and prevent countless production incidents. But we're just getting started. Our vision is to make Kubernetes as reliable and self-healing as modern cloud infrastructure should be.
