Frequently Asked Questions

What's the best way to reach your sales team?

The fastest way is to book a demo using our booking system. If you prefer email, reach out to sales@k8sagent.io. We usually respond within 24 hours.

Do you offer phone support?

For Hosted and Enterprise customers, yes. Community/Open Source users have access to our GitHub discussions and community forum.

What's your typical response time?

Sales inquiries: 24 hours. Enterprise support: 1 hour. Community forum: 2-3 days (volunteer-based). Premium support: within 30 minutes.

Can I get a custom demo?

Absolutely! Book a demo and tell us about your specific use case. Our team will customize the demo to show how K8s Agent solves your challenges.

Do you have a community forum?

Yes! We have an active Slack community and GitHub discussions where you can connect with other users, share experiences, and get help.

How can I provide product feedback?

We'd love to hear it! You can submit feedback through this form, on our GitHub roadmap, or directly to our product team at product@k8sagent.io.
